Game rules
Host rules Players rules

For host

As a host you should pick one of the words given to you by the game and try to draw it so other players could guess what was your word.

Main restrictions:

  • Do not draw letters of your word
  • Do not draw prohibited images
  • Draw only something connected to your word
  • Have fun!

For players

As a player you should wait for the host to choose his word and then try to guess what is being drawn by your host.

Main restrictions:

  • Do not spam in the chat
  • Do not type prohibited words and messages
  • Do not type before you think
  • Have fun!
